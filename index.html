Data Science & Machine Learning

Carbon Statistical Modeling - DISC Agri-AI Challenge

Tech Stack: Python, Scikit-Learn, Google Colab

Data and results are confidential

Scikit-Learn models trained and tested on agricultural data to over 80% accuracy in predicting carbon fluxes. Ensured data quality despite faulty readings, presented insights to 5+ PhD Scientists.

Technical Approach

  • Built Correlation Matrices and Structural Equation Models (SEM)
  • Created an Excel sheet with a variety of feature subsets by referencing the matrices and SEM
  • Developed scripts for easy switching of feature sets with immediate output generation
  • Models used: LinearRegression, RandomForest, SVM, and DecisionTree from SK-Learn
  • Focused on targeting specific flux values for estimation
